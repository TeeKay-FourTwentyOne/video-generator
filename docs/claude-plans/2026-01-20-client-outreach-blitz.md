# War Room Sprint: Client Outreach Blitz

**Duration:** 1 hour
**Goal:** Send 3 cold pitches to potential paying clients

## Why This

- $0 income, 4-month runway
- You have proof of work (577K views, viral December content, working AI pipeline)
- Outreach is the only action that could lead to income tonight

## The Hour

### Phase 1: Target List (15 min)
Identify 5 potential clients:
- Small video/marketing agencies
- Content creators who post frequently
- Local businesses with weak video presence
- Anyone who commented positively on your viral content

### Phase 2: Write Pitches (25 min)
Template structure:
1. One line about their work (personalized)
2. "I build AI video pipelines"
3. Link to your channel / best video
4. "577K views, December viral run"
5. Specific offer: what you could make for them
6. CTA: "Interested? Let's talk."

Write 3 personalized versions.

### Phase 3: Send (15 min)
- Send all 3 tonight
- Use whatever platform they're on (email, DM, LinkedIn)

### Phase 4: Log (5 min)
Add to `data/warroom/briefing.md`:
- Who you contacted
- What you sent
- Date

## Files to Update
- `data/warroom/briefing.md` - Add Leads/Contacts section entries

## Success Criteria
- 3 pitches sent before sprint ends
- Logged in briefing.md

## Verification
- Check sent folder / DM history
- Verify briefing.md has entries
